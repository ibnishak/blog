Criteria for Personal Knowledge Base

October 03, 2020   Riz   27 s  

Essential criteria.

  1. Mobile first.
  2. Privacy.
  3. Cross platform.
    • At least Linux and Android must be supported.
  4. Scalable.
  5. Ability to cross link notes.

Desired criteria.

  1. Fast search and navigation.
  2. Semi automation of organization.
  3. Bottom up organization.
  4. Fast input.
  5. Least effort for initial setup, but extendable according to personal requirements.

Ideal criteria

  1. Complete portability
    • The question to consider is: “If I have to run and all I can take is a pendrive, can I take my pkb with me and how effectively can I use it?”
  2. Visualization of relationships between notes as a mind-map.
  3. Web-clipper add-on for browser.
  4. Secure cloud backups
  5. Tool-chain for easy publishing
